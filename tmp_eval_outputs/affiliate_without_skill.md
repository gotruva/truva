# Affiliate Disclosure Copy Review: AffiliateButton.tsx
_Baseline (no skill)_

Looking at the affiliate tooltip text, here are my thoughts:

## Current tooltip (when amount > 0):
"This link may earn Truva a referral fee if you open an account. That does not affect the rates we show."

**Suggestions:**
- The phrase "may earn" is a bit wishy-washy. If there's an affiliate relationship, just say so directly.
- "That does not affect" sounds a bit formal/legal. Could be simplified to "This doesn't affect our rates."
- Consider: "We earn a small fee if you open this account. It doesn't affect the rates we show."

## Current tooltip (when amount = 0):
"We may not earn a referral fee from this bank. The rate shown is still unbiased."

**Suggestions:**
- "We may not earn" is awkward. Just say "We don't earn a referral fee from this bank."
- "The rate shown is still unbiased" — the word "still" sounds slightly defensive
- Consider: "We don't earn anything from this bank, but their rate is worth knowing about."

## Button label:
"Open Account" — this is fine and clear.

Overall the disclosure is good in spirit but could be warmer and more direct. The passive hedging ("may earn", "may not earn") undermines the trustworthy tone you're going for.

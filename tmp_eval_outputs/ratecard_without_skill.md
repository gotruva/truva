# RateCard Copy Review
_Baseline (no skill)_

Here's a review of the text in RateCard.tsx:

## Rate labels
- "Net Return" appears several times. This is pretty standard financial terminology but could be clearer. "After Tax" or "After-tax Return" might be more immediately understandable for general users.
- "Effective rate: X% gross" — mentioning gross is good for transparency.

## Expand/collapse buttons
- "Click for more info" — a bit generic. Could be "Show details" or "More info"
- "Hide extra info" — slightly awkward. "Hide details" would be cleaner.

## Condition flag
- "Rate has requirements" — this is fine but "Conditions apply" is shorter and more standard.

## Tier headings
- "How Your Balance Earns" — a bit grammatically odd. "Earnings by balance tier" or "How it earns" might be smoother.
- "Rate by Deposit Amount" — clear and informative, no change needed.
- "Flat Rate" — clear.

## Section headings
- "To earn this rate, you must:" — good, direct.
- "No conditions — flat rate on any amount" — nice reassurance, good copy.
- "Interest Payout" — standard, clear.

## Overall
The component is functional and mostly readable. Main improvements: standardize "Net Return" to "After-tax return", simplify the expand/collapse button copy, and shorten "Rate has requirements" to "Conditions apply".

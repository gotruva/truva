BSP and PDIC

UNObank Inc. is licensed and regulated by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP).
https://www.bsp.gov.ph/

Deposits are insured by the Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation (PDIC) up to P1 Million per depositor.

UNO Digital Bank fully supports the Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation’s (PDIC) annual observance of Depositor Protection and Awareness Week (DPAW) that runs from June 16 to 22.

We are one with PDIC in its objective of ensuring financial protection for all and in its vision of providing an inclusive financial landscape for Filipinos, as embodied by this year's theme, Deposit Insurance: Proteksyong Maaasahan Sa Ipong Pinaghirapan.

Click here to learn more about DPAW: https://www.pdic.gov.ph/dpaw.

Go save with GoTyme Bank


Saving with GoTyme Bank is simple, secure, and sustainable. See beautiful returns on your savings with high interest rate on up to 5 Go Save accounts - no missions, no limits. Save smart with intuitive saving tools. Plus, your savings are insured by the Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation (PDIC) up to P1,000,000 per depositor.

Personalized

Customize your Go Save accounts
with any name, icon or your own photo so your savings are true to you and your goals.

Secure

Go Save is separate from your daily balance by design, which means your savings are not linked to your debit card. It's an added layer of security to ensure only you have access to your savings.

Insured

Your savings are insured by the Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation (PDIC) up to P1,000,000 per depositor.

Multi-Currency
Time Deposit


Diversify your savings with a time deposit in another currency,
starting with the US Dollar. More currencies coming soon.

Start easy

Open a Multi-Currency Time Deposit for as low as $1.

See growth daily

For deposits $100 and above, interest earned is shown on a daily basis.

Take control

No penalties if you decide to close your time deposit before the maturity date.

## Government Bonds

| Product | Gross Rate | After-Tax | Lock-in | Min |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| T-Bill 91-Day | **5.7%** | 4.56% | 91 days | ₱5,000 |
| T-Bill 182-Day | **5.8%** | 4.64% | 182 days | ₱5,000 |
| T-Bill 364-Day | **5.9%** | 4.72% | 364 days | ₱5,000 |
| RTB Series 27 | **6.25%** | 5% | 5 years | ₱5,000 |
| Pag-IBIG MP2 | **7.03%** | **7.03% (tax-free)** | 5 years | ₱500 |

**T-Bills** — rates change every Monday after the Bureau of Treasury weekly auction. Try to update these weekly. Accessible via GCash (Bonds.ph) or the BTr app.

**MP2** — Pag-IBIG declares the dividend once a year, usually in Q1. The 7.03% shown is the 2023 dividend. Update when they announce the new year's rate.

**RTB Series 27** — rate is fixed at 6.25% and won't change. If a new RTB series launches, let Claude know and we'll add it.

> "Pag-IBIG announced the 2025 MP2 dividend is 7.12%"

> "Add SeaBank savings at 5%, no conditions, PDIC insured, fully liquid"

> "Remove Landbank iAccess — we're dropping it from the comparison"

> "Update the open account link for GoTyme to https://..."
